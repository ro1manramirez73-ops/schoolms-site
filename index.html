Questions

Frequently asked

Does SchoolMS work without internet? +
Yes. After install, the app runs completely offline. Staff access it through your local Wi-Fi — no internet required after the initial download.
Is there a monthly subscription or renewal fee? +
No. Pay once and own that version forever. Future major updates may be available at a discounted upgrade price, but the version you buy never expires.
How many students can I add? +
Unlimited students in all plans. Staff account limits only apply to login accounts (teachers, accountants, front desk), not student records.
Can staff and parents access it from home? +
Yes — using your own Cloudflare Tunnel (free). A setup script included with SchoolMS configures everything automatically and gives your system a real HTTPS URL like school.yourdomain.com.
Is it available in Spanish? +
Yes. Full English and Spanish support is built in. Switch language from the sidebar with one click — no restart needed. Ideal for schools in Honduras, Guatemala, and other Spanish-speaking countries.
What countries is SchoolMS available in? +
SchoolMS is used by schools across the Caribbean and Central America — Bahamas, Belize, Honduras, Guatemala, Jamaica, Trinidad and Tobago, Dominican Republic, and more. It works on any Windows computer worldwide.
Can I import my existing student data? +
Yes. The Import Data page accepts any CSV file exported from Excel. The system auto-detects your column names — it understands common variants like "first name", "firstname", "Mother-Name", and more.
Can I get a refund? +
If SchoolMS cannot be installed or run on your computer within 14 days of purchase, contact us for a full refund — no questions asked. We'll try to resolve it remotely first.
