FAQ

Questions we get asked most.

Does SchoolMS work without internet? +
Yes. After installation, the app runs completely offline. Staff access it through your local Wi-Fi network — no internet connection required after the initial download.
Is there a monthly fee or renewal cost? +
No. Pay once and own that version forever. There are no monthly fees, no per-student charges, and no renewal costs. Future major versions may be available at a discounted upgrade price.
How many students can I add? +
Unlimited students in all plans. Staff account limits only apply to login accounts — teachers, accountants, front desk staff — not to student records.
Can staff and parents access it from home? +
Yes — using a Cloudflare Tunnel (free). A setup script included with SchoolMS configures everything automatically and gives your system a real HTTPS URL accessible from anywhere in the world.
Can I import my existing student data? +
Yes. The Import Data page accepts any CSV file exported from Excel. The system auto-detects your column names and handles common variants like "first name", "firstname", "Mother-Name", and more.
What countries is SchoolMS available in? +
SchoolMS is used by schools across the Caribbean and Central America — Bahamas, Belize, Honduras, Guatemala, Jamaica, Trinidad and Tobago, Dominican Republic, and more. It works on any Windows computer worldwide.
Can I get a refund if it does not work? +
If SchoolMS cannot be installed or run on your computer within 14 days of purchase, contact us for a full refund — no questions asked. We will try to resolve the issue remotely first.
What is new in version 2.5? +
Bulk CSV import for students and staff, a built-in AI Assistant powered by Claude, a Walk-in Kiosk module for tablet self-service intake, in-app SMTP email setup, a first-time setup guide, and desktop shortcut auto-creation on install.
